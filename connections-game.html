Connections Game

Welcome to the Connections Game! This game challenges you to find groups of related words.

How to Play:

  1. You'll see a 4x4 grid of words.
  2. Your goal is to find four groups of four related words.
  3. Click on four words that you think belong together.
  4. Press the "Submit Selection" button to check your guess.
  5. If correct, the words will turn green and can't be selected again.
  6. If incorrect, try a different combination.
  7. Continue until you've found all four groups!
