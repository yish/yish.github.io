OpenStax Mathematics Guide

Review these core principles of probability from Section 7.5 to make calculated decisions.

Theoretical Probability:

Calculated based on sample space size: $P(E) = \frac{n(E)}{n(S)}$, where $n(S)$ is the total outcomes.

Expected Value:

Average outcome: $E(\text{Net}) = P(\text{win}) \times (\text{Reward} - \text{Cost}) + P(\text{lose}) \times (-\text{Cost})$. Which simplifies directly to: $E(\text{Net}) = [P(\text{win}) \times \text{Reward}] - \text{Cost}$.
